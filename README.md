 # New Garments  
  
​Share — your spark,  
Do not hide it from the world!  
Let it be, in the moment — clumsy,  
Let it be — at times, ungraceful...  
  
​When suddenly, life —  
Alone, became clear,  
Share your warmth —  
With everyone in the crowd...  
  
​Do not hide your feelings,  
Having accepted — a bold challenge,  
To manifest — in everyone,  
For them, you are — a beacon of hope...  
  
​You do not want, as before...  
Everything — will not be as it was!  
And already, the choice is made —  
To change all the garments.....  
  
​☆ Body  
... No longer are there —  
Long inhalations of restraint  
And suppression...  
Sharp exhalations —  
Of fears and doubts — are absent...  
There is a single, smooth breath —  
Without arrhythmic emotions,  
Without separation,  
Without suffering...  
The breath of the skin —  
Continues the breath of the body,  
Uniting with each other,  
Each — in its own way,  
But — in a single time...  
Movements — a ripple of air,  
Over the scorching sands of the desert —  
Fluid and smooth,  
Monumental and strong...  
In them, the power —  
Of all winds, hurricanes,  
In them — the power of volume,  
Of all waters and oceans...  
  
​☆ Voice  
... From the first sound of the inhalation,  
Before the word —  
Everyone around falls still,  
In humble attention...  
The timbre — with the purity of all hertz,  
Touches through the hearing —  
Hearts, beating nearby...  
No longer are there barriers for it  
And superior forces —  
To drown out, to distort...  
There is only dependence  
And a desire —  
In its healing energies,  
To infinitely abide  
And live...  
...Silence — is Gold,  
The indestructible guard  
Of the priceless treasure —  
Of the new voice...  
  
​☆ Gaze  
... The Tip of the Iceberg —  
Of Absolute Willpower.  
Capable of stopping —  
Any threats and storms...  
Capable — of extinguishing fire  
And anger — inside anyone...  
Capable — of returning to oneself,  
Beyond names  
And any conditioning...  
The Gaze — of Space itself,  
Through one's own eyes!  
Clearly seeing — all causes  
And probable continuations...  
Into the Soul — of Anyone,  
Openly gliding...  
A Gaze — filling  
And guiding,  
All-understanding  
And healing...  
Giving — hope,  
Confidence, courage  
And cutting off —  
Pride and ignorance...  
A Gaze — through the core,  
Of those nearby,  
Seeing the worst and the best —  
As equals...  
Seeing — knowing,  
Wisely forgiving,  
Quietly — the Worthy ones,  
Warning...  
    
​...In the new garments —    
There is no pathos, no screams.     
There is only comfort    
And silk — tactility...    
A riddle, an intrigue,    
An alluring mystery...    
Charm, gracefulness    
And the modesty of a Monk...    
..The confidence of a Mage,    
The strength of a Warrior,    
The healer's image    
And symbols of Peace...    
In them — one can be shod,    
Or one can be barefoot,    
From now on, open are —    
All doors, paths...    
  
​P.S.: Peace — to all Worlds and Planets!  
Peace — to all intelligent Beings!  
Inside Themselves — with themselves  
And Everyone, who with them,  
Around them —  
Is Alive... ❤️
